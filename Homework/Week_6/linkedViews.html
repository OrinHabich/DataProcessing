The number of freelancers, per age group, per year, per gender.

By Orin Habich 10689508

The number of freelancers in the Netherlands is growing. These data visualisations show how the proportions, and the male/ female ratio is changing. Hovering over the barcharts updates the piechart for the specific year and age group. Differentiating between year, age group and gender allows for a more profound analysis of the data. One thing we can tell from these charts is that the ratio male/ female freelancers is more equal under young adults, then in higher age groups.
